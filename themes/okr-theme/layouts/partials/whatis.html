What are OKRs?

OKRs, or Objectives and Key Results, is a goal-setting technique that enables teams to focus on achieving very ambitious goals and objectives. These objectives are measured by quantifiable Key Results. Scores range from 0 to 1 for a key result, while the "sweet spot" or ideal score for an objective is 0.6-0.7 -- any higher means that the objective is too easy, and any lower means that the objective is too ambitious. Scores are set that way so that teams can completely maximize their performance while also being able to work outside their comfort zone.

Ideally three objectives each with 3 key results.

Reviewed at least every two months for progress updates.

0.6 - 0.7 as ideal scores for primary objectives.
